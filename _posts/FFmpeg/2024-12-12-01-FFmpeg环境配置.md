## 1 Window配置

1. 打开FFmpeg网站：[https://www.ffmpeg.org/download.html#build-windows](https://www.ffmpeg.org/download.html#build-windows)

2. 点击 `Windows builds by BtbN` 下载编译好的程序和动态库。

3. 添加程序路径到系统环境变量PATH
